Overview

Version 1
Giorgio Buccellati – March 2021

     This website serves as a programmatic introductory hub to the cluster of six websites listed on the home page.

     Part 1. CONCEPTS provides a brief review of the major theoretical issues which the cluster aims to bring into focus, in particular with regard to the nature of digitality and the view of a website as a structural whole.
     Part 2. COROLLARIES develops some points that relate to some particular theoretical aspects of the project.
     Part 3 deals with the SUBSTANTIVE aim of the project, describing how the websites can be used as tools for bibliographical research.
      Part 4 deals, analogously, with METHODOLOGICAL aim of the project, showing how the various components of the websites implement concretely the notion of digital discourse, which is the primary methodological goal of the cluster.
     Part 5. THE RECORD contains the bibliographical data that are related to theoretical issues taken up in this programmatic website.
     Part 6. MECHANISMS presents the infrastructure that is in place to keep the system functioning.

     The left handside bar gives the site map, and serves as a Table of Contents that provides the reader, at all times, with a sense of the internal unity and structure of the digital discourse as developed within the website.
