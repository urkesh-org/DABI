Mesopotamian
religion and spirituality

with reference to the biblical world

Editor: Giorgio Buccellati
Associate Editor: Jonah Lynch

     Analytical coverage of the bibliography on Mesopotamian religion, with a special emphasis on the spiritual dimension and on its relationship, and contrast, with its biblical counterpart.
 
     The structure of the website organizes the data around a central narrative, which at the moment is the one of the book When on High the Heavens.
 
     Extended summaries, critical reviews, and topical essays are interlaced with each other and with the main narrative.
 
     An open forum includes a number of participants who contribute on a regular basis to the website.
